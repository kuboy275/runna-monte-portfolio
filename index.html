About Runa Mont

Bio

Born in Ho Chi Minh City, Kim Trang as know as Runa Mont is a sound designer/ DJ/ Music Producer academically trained in Denmark and Viet Nam whose work explores virtual reality and experimentation. Her compositions are condensed with alternative textures and raw emotions, all combined in an exciting atmosphere. Her projects feature electronic improvisation, different sonic textures created from technology, and an aim to create an artistic platform of human connections.

Runa is the vision behind the recent Pure Dance Night at Piu Piu, where she fundraised for a local nature conservatory through music.
